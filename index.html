About US

Happy Hostel is a family business that has been home for travellers and backpackers here in London for the past 17 years. Our founder intended to create a place which will be more than just a place to stay in a big city. Following his passion, he has created a Happy Hostel, where every guest is welcomed with a warm and friendly atmosphere. The hostel offers a variety of amenities such as a home-made breakfast, hot shower, and a clean bed.
